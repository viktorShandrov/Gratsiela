THE CTEATOR

Gratsiela Ivanova

Artist, Writer & Designer

I am an artist by profession and vocation. I graduated from high school with a degree in Painting and a professional qualification in Artist, and later a higher education in Interior Design. Over the years, I have created and sold over 350 paintings that have found their home and brought emotion and inspiration to their owners. Art is part of my life – it is in my blood and is my everyday life. In addition to being an artist, I also act as an author. I wrote a book about life, in which I share personal insights, experiences and reflections inspired by my life path. I believe that every work of art carries energy, history and message. For me, creativity is not just a profession – it is a way of life and a natural expression of my essence.

My work revolves around texture, composition, and color psychology. I believe in establishing a dialogue between organic paint textures and clean, classical typography. My signature color palette—burgundy canvas, cyclamen accents, grey, and champagne tones—is a reflection of this philosophy: deeply emotive yet structured.

Whether I am layering heavy body acrylics on canvas, binding a new art guide, or constructing a monogram, my goal remains constant: to design beautiful, premium experiences that resonate deeply. "Where art speaks beyond words."
